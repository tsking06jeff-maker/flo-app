Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains exactly what data Flo collects, how we use it, who we share it with, and what control you have over it.

1. What We Collect

When you use Flo, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data — your email address, name, and encrypted password (managed by Supabase Auth)
  • Financial data — income, savings goals, budget categories, and transactions that you manually enter
  • AI interaction data — questions you ask the Flo AI advisor and the financial summary sent to generate responses
  • Usage data — login timestamps, feature usage, and session information for service improvement
  • Device data — browser type and general device information for compatibility purposes

We do not collect your bank credentials, credit card numbers, or connect to any financial institutions directly. All financial data in Flo is entered manually by you.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Flo service
  • Generate AI-powered financial insights using your budget and transaction data
  • Send transactional emails such as password resets and security alerts
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the product (anonymized)

3. AI & Your Financial Data

When you use Ask Flo AI, a summary of your financial data (income, spending, budget categories) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is used only to produce your response and is not retained by Anthropic beyond the duration of the request, per their data processing policies.

We do not use your personal financial data to train AI models. We do not share identifiable financial data with any third party for advertising or analytics purposes.

4. What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal or financial data to any third party
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use your data to train AI or machine learning models
  • We do not access your bank accounts, investments, or external financial services

5. Third-Party Services

Flo relies on the following trusted third parties to operate:

  • Supabase — database storage and authentication. Your financial data and account credentials are stored here with row-level security.
  • Anthropic — AI response generation. A financial summary is sent per request and not retained.
  • Vercel — application hosting and serverless functions.
  • Stripe (if applicable) — payment processing for subscriptions. We do not store full card details.

Each provider operates under their own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your data is protected by:

  • Row-level security (RLS) — you can only access your own data, enforced at the database level
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Supabase Auth for secure password hashing — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Server-side API key management — your AI API keys are never exposed client-side

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights over your data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data at any time from account settings
  • Portability — request an export of your financial data in a readable format
  • Objection — object to specific processing of your data where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the app. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, all personal data, financial data, and transaction history is permanently and irreversibly deleted within 30 days. Subscription billing records may be retained longer as required by law.

9. Cookies

Flo uses minimal cookies and local storage — primarily for session management and user preferences such as dark/light mode. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Flo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notice at least 14 days before changes take effect. Your continued use of Flo after that date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, want to exercise your data rights, or have a privacy concern, please contact us through the app. We take all privacy inquiries seriously and will respond as promptly as possible.
