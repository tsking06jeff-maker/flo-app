Common questions
Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No — you can explore Flo free without entering payment info. A card is only required when you choose to start the Pro trial.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
What happens after my free trial?
You'll be charged $9.99/month after the 7-day trial unless you cancel before it ends. We'll send you a reminder before the trial expires.
Is Flo a financial advisor?
No. Flo provides general budgeting tools and AI-generated commentary for informational purposes only. For professional advice, please consult a qualified financial advisor.
