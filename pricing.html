Common questions
Do I need a credit card for the free trial?
No — you can explore Flo free without entering any payment info. A card is only required when you choose to upgrade to Pro.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period — no questions asked.
Is Flo a financial advisor?
No. Flo provides general budgeting tools and AI-generated financial commentary for informational purposes only. It is not a licensed financial advisor and does not make personalized investment recommendations. For professional advice, please consult a qualified financial advisor.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. Your data is protected by row-level security — only you can access your own data. All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. We never sell your data or share it with advertisers.
What happens if my payment fails?
We'll notify you by email and give you a grace period to update your payment method. Your account won't be immediately restricted — we'll always give you a heads up first.
