This Privacy Policy explains how Flo ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our Service. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices. Please read this carefully.

We collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

We do not collect your bank account credentials, credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, government IDs, or connect directly to any financial institution. All financial data in Flo is entered manually by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Flo Service
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Generate AI-powered budgeting commentary using your financial data
  • Process subscription payments and manage billing
  • Send transactional emails (payment receipts, security alerts, trial expiry reminders)
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Improve the Service through anonymized, aggregated usage analysis
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, do not sell it to third parties, and do not use it to train AI models.

3. AI Features & Your Financial Data

When you use Ask Flo AI, a summary of your financial data (income, spending totals, budget categories) is transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This data is used only to generate your specific response. Per Anthropic's API data handling policies, this data is not stored by Anthropic beyond the duration of the API request and is not used to train AI models.

We transmit only aggregated financial summaries — not your full transaction history or personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for the response. You can review what is sent by reviewing your budget data in the app.

5. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Row-level security (RLS): Enforced at the database level — you can only read and write your own data
  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Password security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt via Supabase Auth — never stored in plain text
  • API key protection: All sensitive API keys (Anthropic, Stripe) are stored as server-side environment variables and never exposed to the browser
  • Service role isolation: Administrative database operations (e.g. subscription updates from webhooks) use a separate service role key not accessible to users

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data, financial data, transactions, categories, and budgets are permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Your Stripe subscription is cancelled immediately upon account deletion
  • Billing records may be retained for up to 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be linked to you

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data at any time from account settings
  • Portability: Request an export of your financial data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data where applicable law permits
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the app. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

Flo uses minimal browser storage:

  • LocalStorage: Used for theme preference (dark/light mode) and session tokens managed by Supabase Auth
  • SessionStorage: Used to cache subscription status during your browsing session to reduce database queries
  • Cookies: Supabase Auth may set authentication cookies for session management

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. We do not use cookies to track your activity across other websites.

9. Children's Privacy

Flo's subscription service is intended for users 18 and older. The free tier is available to users 13 and older with parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has used our Service, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

Flo is operated in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer. We take steps to ensure your data receives appropriate protection regardless of where it is processed.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or in-app notice at least 14 days before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your data rights, or have a privacy concern, please contact us through the app. We take all privacy inquiries seriously and will respond within 5 business days.
